OGC seeks public comment on the adoption of openEO as a Community Standard (phase 2)

Written on April 5, 2025 by Matthias Mohr.

OGC seeks public comment on the potential adoption of openEO as an OGC Community Standard. Comments are due by April 21, 2025.

For more details, please read the press release that was published by OGC: OGC looks to adopt openEO as Community Standard; Seeking Public Comment before moving to vote (opens new window)

We'll keep you updated about the progress.
